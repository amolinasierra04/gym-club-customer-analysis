# Methodology

## Overview

This project follows a structured analytical approach designed to simulate a real-world business analysis scenario. The methodology focuses on transforming raw data into actionable insights for decision-making.

---

## Tools Used

- SQL (SQLite): Data exploration, transformation, and analysis
- Power BI: Data visualization and dashboard creation

---

## Analytical Process

### 1. Data Understanding

- Initial exploration of the dataset structure
- Identification of key variables (demographic, behavioral, and service-related)
- Detection of missing values and data inconsistencies

---

### 2. Data Cleaning and Preparation

- Validation of null values and their business meaning
- Standardization of column names
- Creation of derived variables where necessary (e.g., age groups, usage categories)
- Preparation of data for analysis through SQL queries

---

### 3. Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)

The dataset is explored through SQL queries to identify patterns and relationships across different dimensions:

- Customer demographics (age, gender)
- Membership types (Standard vs Premium)
- Gym usage (visits per week, session duration)
- Service usage (group lessons, personal training, sauna, drinks)

---

### 4. Comparative Analysis

Key comparisons are performed to uncover meaningful differences:

- Standard vs Premium members
- Users vs non-users of specific services
- High-frequency vs low-frequency users

The goal is to identify behavioral patterns that can inform business decisions.

---

### 5. Customer Segmentation

Customers are grouped based on behavioral and service usage patterns. Segmentation is not purely statistical, but based on business logic, allowing for more interpretable and actionable results.

Examples of segmentation dimensions:
- Frequency of visits
- Use of additional services
- Type of membership

---

### 6. Insight Generation

The analysis focuses on extracting insights that go beyond descriptive statistics. Each finding is evaluated in terms of its potential business impact.

---

### 7. Visualization

Key results are presented using Power BI dashboards, designed to:
- Be easily understood by non-technical stakeholders
- Highlight the most relevant insights
- Support decision-making

---

## Approach Philosophy

This project prioritizes:
- Clarity over complexity
- Business relevance over technical sophistication
- Actionable insights over purely descriptive analysis
